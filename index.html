Empowering You to Take Control of Your Retirement

Superannuation Warehouse is an accounting firm that focuses on delivering accounting and auditing services tailored specifically for SMSF Trustees

Simple setups to get started with the SMSF Expert

SW will set up your SMSF

register it with the ATO, and secure both an ABN and a TFN.

Open a Bank Account

Open a bank or deposit account with any bank of your choice.

Transfer Existing Funds

Transfer your existing superannuation from your retail funds into your new SMSF.

Share Trading Account

You can establish a bank account with any bank or credit union.

Set Up Direct Debit

After your fund is established, SW will send you instructions for setting up Direct Debit payments.

Scale, Grow and Optimize Your SMSF

Success with SMSFs demands the ability to scale, grow, and optimize your fund in response to evolving needs. Our customized SMSF solutions are engineered to help you fully capitalize on your retirement savings, providing the necessary tools, strategies, and expertise to drive sustained growth and enhance fund performance.

Why choose us

Effortless End-of-Year Data Handling

At the end of each financial year, simply provide us with soft copies of your SMSF bank and share trading account statements. We take care of loading the data into our accounting system, preparing your accounts, and handling your SMSF tax returns. Our process is fully electronic and paperless for your convenience.

Investment Freedom for Trustees

You have full control over where and how you invest your SMSF funds. We handle all the annual administrative functions, such as preparing accounts, calculating taxes, arranging audits, and submitting tax returns, so you can focus on making the best investment decisions.

Advanced Package for Added Flexibility

If you prefer to use other service providers, our Advanced Package is available for just $100. With data provided in a consistent electronic format, we can offer a comprehensive administration service at a competitive monthly fee, giving you the flexibility to choose while ensuring your SMSF is well-managed.
